1. What K6/QS3 Is

A discrete quantum field theory program. The substrate is the complete graph K6 (6 vertices, 15 edges, cyclomatic complexity 10) combined with the quaternionic 3-sphere QS3 (S³ with quaternionic structure, isomorphic to SU(2)). Two copies of QS3 are connected at a wedge basepoint: QS3₁ (quarks, color) and QS3₂ (leptons, colorless). The K₃,₃ bipartite subgraph (3+3 vertices, 9 inter-group edges) carries SU(3) color. The Hopf fibration S³→S² with fiber S¹ is intrinsic to QS3 and carries U(1) electromagnetism.

K6/QS3 is also a model of spacetime: each cell is a deformable 3-sphere, and the foam packing of these cells is curved spacetime. Metric deformation = gravitational field. Equilibrium packing (minimizing crease area) = Einstein equations in discrete form.

Guiding principle: no dimensionless constants inserted by hand. All constants must be derivable from K6/QS3 geometry. The framework is falsifiable.

18. Primordial Symmetries (Paper 7) UPDATED

Parsimony Axiom: "What is not necessary, is not." — purely ontological, no causality assumed. Necessity and existence are coextensive. Axiom of Choice is excluded by design: no selection from a set of alternatives is possible — alternatives do not exist. K6/QS3 was preferred; we will never know why.

Two primordial symmetries:

  • L0: A=A, A≠0. The Aristotelian identity. ℤ₂ (left↔right exchange of identity). The indeterminacy scale of this ℤ₂ is ℏ.
  • L1: At any moment, exactly one of: (explore state space) XOR (seek dynamic stability), at frequencies Ω₁ and Ω₂ respectively, with Ω₁=Ω₂=ℏ. ℤ₂ (Ω₁↔Ω₂ exchange). The Paul trap analogy is exact: alternation at Ω₁=Ω₂ produces dynamic confinement — neither frozen nor diffuse. Heisenberg uncertainty ΔxΔp≥ℏ/2 is the observable residue of the XOR structure in the spacetime epoch. Gauge fields arise from a transient Ω₁≠Ω₂ at the logic→spacetime transition — L1 itself permitted the imbalance as the least-action option for state-space expansion; Ω₁=Ω₂ restored rapidly after spacetime emerged. The gauge field is the structural record of that event.
  • Together: L0×L1 ≅ ℤ₂×ℤ₂ (Klein four-group). Both ℤ₂ factors calibrated by the same ℏ. Minimal non-cyclic foundation. The Hodge star is native to this — EM duality is primordial.

The entailment hierarchy (not a temporal sequence): The chain s=0 → K3 → K4 → S² → Hopf S³→S² → QS3₁∨QS3₂ → trefoil surgery → K₃,₃ → K6/QS3 foam is a logical entailment hierarchy, not a causal or temporal sequence. It should be read as Groups→Modules→Algebras is read: each level is logically entailed by the previous under the parsimony axiom. Irreversibility and causality belong to the spacetime epoch and are absent here. "Emergence" in Paper 7's title is a concession to language; "entailment chain" is more precise.

Uniqueness — Gödel boundary: K6/QS3 is a unique termination of the necessity hierarchy — each step was entailed given the previous. Whether it is the unique termination is not decidable from within the system. Whether the symmetry broke in a unique way is undecidable. This is the correct epistemic boundary.

Black holes as topological imprints: After Dehn surgery, s sits permanently on the boundary, biasing L1. Pre-geometric anisotropy develops. Topological pinching — regions of foam whose holonomies become trapped — is L1 in action: the boundary singularity asserting itself into the topology. Primordial black holes are the later physical manifestation of this pre-geometric necessity, not accidents of gravitational collapse.
